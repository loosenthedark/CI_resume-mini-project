Conferences and Meetups Attended

My groups and societies

Python Ireland

Python Ireland is the Irish organisation representing the various chapters of Python users. We organise meetups and events for software developers, students, academics and anyone who wants to learn the language. One of our aims is to help grow and diversify the Python community in Ireland. We also develop and foster links with other Python-based communities overseas.

Code For Ireland

Code For Ireland follows in the footsteps of Code For America, which was founded in 2009. Its mission is to develop innovative and sustainable solutions to real-world problems faced by communities across Ireland, by fostering collaborations between technologists and civic-minded individuals, businesses and public-sector organisations.

Full Stack NY

This meetup is an event where web designers and developers can come together to learn from and teach each other about emerging technologies. While speakers at Full Stack focus primarily on AngularJS, React, Node.js, hapi, Express, plain ol' JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, we encourage all local web professionals to join us for vivid discussions around technology across the stack in any language.

IoT Nation

IoT Nation's mission is to inspire, engage, and connect entrepreneurs, investors, professionals, & enthusiasts worldwide who are active in the Internet of Things (IoT). We welcome those who want to learn about this emerging field which is fascinating as IoT makes use of a diverse range of technologies and has the promise to impact a vast number of vertical markets.
